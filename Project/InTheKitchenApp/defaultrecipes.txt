<recipe>
<title>Chicken and Mushroom Pie</title>
<description>
A tasty British dish. This is just what you need on a cold night. Serve with creamy mashed potatoes.
</description>
<notes>
Use chicken thights for a better taste.
Use shortcrut pastry (not puff pastry) if chicken pie is to be saved and served cold.
</notes>
<photoname>Chicken-bacon-and-mushroom-plate-pie.jpg</photoname>
<preptime>45</preptime>
<cookingtime>30</cookingtime>
<persons>4 - 6</persons>
<sugar>6</sugar>
<energy>855</energy>
<protein>55</protein>
<carbohydrates>57</carbohydrates>
<fat>47</fat>
<natrium>3</natrium>
<fibres>1</fibres>

<mealtype>Kip</mealtype>
<theme>Daags,Avond maaltijd</theme>
<steps>
STEP 1
Heat the oil in a large, non-stick frying pan. Season the chicken thighs and fry for 5-8 mins until golden brown, turning occasionally. You may need to do this in two batches, depending on the size of your pan.

STEP 2
Lift the chicken onto a plate and tip the bacon pieces into the pan. Fry for 5 mins until crisp. Add the onion, mushrooms and thyme sprigs, then fry on a high heat for another 3 mins until the onions start to colour. Tip the flour into the pan and cook, stirring, for 1 min.

STEP 3
With the pan off the heat, gradually stir or whisk in the chicken stock, followed by the milk, then add the chicken back to the pan.

STEP 4
Bring to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins. Spoon the filling into a large pie or baking dish (approx 20 x 30cm) with a lip and leave to cool.

STEP 5
Heat the oven to 220C/200C fan/gas 7. On a floured surface, roll the pastry to the thickness of two coins. Cut a long strip as wide as the rim of the pie dish and, using a little of the beaten egg, fix to the edge of the pie dish. Brush with beaten egg, then lift the rest of the pastry over the pie, using the rolling pin to help. Gently press the edges with your fingers and trim with a sharp knife.

STEP 6
Brush lightly with egg to glaze, then bake for 30 mins or until the pastry is risen and dark golden brown.
</steps>
<ingredients>
1 tbsp vegetable oil
8 skinless boneless chicken thighs
8 rashers smoked streaky bacon, cut into large pieces
1 onion, halved and sliced
250g baby button mushrooms
handful of thyme sprigs
2 tbsp plain flour
400ml chicken stock
200ml milk
500g pack fresh puff pastry, or frozen and defrosted
1 egg, beaten</ingredients>

</recipe>

<title>Kippenpoten uit de oven</title>
<description>
Als je nooit kippenpoten maakt omdat het kluiven aan een bot je tegenstaat dan moet je nu toch echt je principes opzij zetten. Probeer het een keer!
Knapperig aan de buitenkant met een pittig korstje en sappig van binnen, klinkt al goed toch!

<steps>
1 Verwarm je oven voor op 200 graden.
2 Spoel de kippenpoten af onder de kraan en dep ze droog met keukenpapier.
3 Meng alle kruiden in een bakje en strooi het over de kippenpoten, verdeel het gelijkmatig.
4 Druppel er een beetje olijfolie over en wrijf het in met je handen.
5 Leg de kippenpoten in een braadslee of ovenschaal en bak in 35 min. gaar in de oven. Draai halverwege om.
